Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Metrowide Appraisals Order Tracker (the "Application") handles information. The Application is a private, internal business tool operated by Metrowide Appraisals ("we," "us") to manage appraisal orders and, with your authorization, create invoices in QuickBooks Online.

Who uses this Application

The Application is used solely by Metrowide Appraisals and its authorized staff. It is not offered to the public and does not create user accounts for third parties.

Information we access

When you connect the Application to QuickBooks Online, and only with your explicit authorization through Intuit's secure sign-in, the Application may access and use the following QuickBooks data on your behalf:

  • Customer records (to find or create the customer on an invoice)
  • Items/services (to reference an appraisal-fee line item)
  • Invoices (to create new invoices)
  • Basic company information (to identify the connected company)

The Application also handles appraisal order details that you enter, such as property address, contact name, email, phone, and fee amount.

How we use information

Information is used only to perform the actions you request — primarily, creating an invoice in your QuickBooks Online company that matches an appraisal order. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any unrelated purpose.

How information is stored and protected

Authorization tokens needed to connect to QuickBooks are stored securely on our server infrastructure (Cloudflare) and are used only to communicate with Intuit's APIs. Sensitive credentials are never exposed publicly. Connections to Intuit are made over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

Sharing of information

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. Data is exchanged only between the Application and Intuit/QuickBooks to carry out the functions you authorize.

Data retention

We retain connection tokens only as long as the QuickBooks connection is active. You may disconnect the Application at any time from your Intuit account's "Connected apps" settings, which revokes its access.

Intuit data

Our use of information received from Intuit APIs adheres to the Intuit Developer policies, and we limit our use of that data to providing the invoicing functionality described above.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be directed to hello@metrowide.co.
